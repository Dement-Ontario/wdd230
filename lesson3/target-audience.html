Target Audience

Target Audience Description

From what I know about chambers of commerce, the target audience would likely be small business owners (or just regular-sized business owners) that live in or near Cambridge, UK. Going from that, they would most likely be in the age range of 25-80 years old, and they would need to have a machine that has a web browser as well as internet access.

Personas

Tony Santino

  • 43 years old
  • Married with 5 kids
  • Runs a small pizza restaurant
  • Would use the site to learn how to advertize his pizza restaurant better

Marcus Smith

  • 26 years old
  • Single
  • Just started a corner store
  • Uses the site to network and learn how to effectively run a business

Scenarios

  • Q) Is it going to rain this week?
    A) The site will show the weather.
  • Q) What happened to Oliver Cromwell?
    A) The site will cover some of Cambridge's history. This includes the fate of Oliver Cromwell.
  • Q) I want to support local business organizations in Cambridge. Where can I find those?
    A) The site will have a list of organizations you can find.
  • Q) This chamber of commerce sounds neat! Where can I join?
    A) The site will have a join page, where you can join the chamber.
  • Q) I don't like the fact about Oliver Cromwell. Where can I ask to get rid of it?
    A) The site will have a contact page where you can send your feedback.
