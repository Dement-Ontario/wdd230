Punting

A popular pastime of Cambridge residents and visitors is going punting down the River Cam, pushing a long boat through the water with a giant pole.

Bonfire Night

Like most in Great Britain, many Cambridge citizens gather every November 5th to celebrate a failed regicide in 1605. Either that, or they just like the fireworks.

Oliver Cromwell

Was Lord Protector of England after the beheading of King Charles I, died of natural causes, was properly buried, and his corpse was dug up and eventually beheaded after his son was overthrown. Apparently, his head is buried at Sidney Sussex College here in Cambridge.

University of Cambridge

The second-oldest university in the english-speaking world, and the third-oldest university still standing, it was made by a group of scholars from the University of Oxford. It is one of the world's most prestegious universities.

Shopping

The shops here in Cambridge are wildly diverse, so it's likely you'll have no trouble fitting your shop in with all the others.
