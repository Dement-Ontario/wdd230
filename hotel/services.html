Services

Specialized Services

Need to Change?

We have free access to changing rooms and bathrooms for if you need to change or freshen up before your temple visit.

Child-Friendly

Our playrooms and low-cost babysitting services will greatly help those who can't bring their children to the temple.

Thematically Sound

We've made sure our reception and sitting areas have the theme of temple history to help you prepare for your temple trip.

Dirty Suit? No Worries

We also have services for washing various kinds of clothes, including a dry-cleaning facility should the need arise.

Family-Finding Made Simpler

Our family search center provides an easy way for you to find family members that are in need of temple ordinances.

Just Married?

You can host your wedding reception at our reception hall, with many customizations to choose from, as well as catering and kitchen access.

Learn More

Temple Missionary?

If you're a full-time temple missionary, you can stay at one of our long-term kitchenette suites for quicker and easier access to the temple.

Free Amenities

Breakfasts

Feeling hungry? Come to our breakfast room for waffles, eggs, bacon, and more. Breakfast goes from 7am-12pm.

Exercise Room

To keep our guests healthy, we have an exercise room with lots of equipment to ensure peak physical performance.

Indoor Pool

Great for swimming in, this pool is big enough both for exercising as well as playing in. Great for kids!

Parking

Each hotel has its very own parking lot, so you don't have to park 2-5 miles away from us.

High-Speed WiFi & Smart TVs

Connect to everyone and everything using our state-of-the-art WiFi. You can watch your favorite shows on your smart TV, too!

Temple Shuttle

Our shuttle can bring you straight to the temple so you don't have to drive there. Great for people that can't drive, or just don't feel like it that day.

Family History

If you're thinking of bringing names to the temple, try our family history consultation and family ordinance card service.
