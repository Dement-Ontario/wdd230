Wedding Reception Hall

General Decoration

We have a wide assortment of decorations to choose from, including but not limited to streamers, balloons, confetti, and hand-crafted wooden statues of the newlyweds, should such a thing be asked for. We also have decorations grouped thematically, so if you want a wedding reception themed after Star Wars, we can do that.

Catering Service

We can provide some simple catering if needed, serving a large range of bite-sized snacks. These snacks include hors d'oeuvres, small fruits, and cocktail sausages.

The Kitchen

The reception hall also includes a large kitchen, where you can prepare all of the food that you want to use for the reception. The kitchen includes cooking utensils, two ovens, one microwave, one sink, and a large refrigerator-freezer hybrid. You can also request other kitchen appliances, such as air fryers, slow cookers, or blenders.

Cleaning Service

After spending the night partying, you can count on us to clean up after you. Unless, of course, you want to help with the cleaning, in which case we won't stop you.
